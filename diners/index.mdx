## The short version

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code">
    Point your phone's camera at the code on the table. Tap the link
    that pops up — no app required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the menu">
    Scroll categories, tap a dish for details and photos, add it to your
    cart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order together (optional)">
    Tap **Order together** to share your cart with the rest of the
    table, so everyone adds their own items to one order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask questions">
    Tap the assistant icon in the header to ask anything about the menu
    — allergens, spice level, recommendations, what goes well together.
  </Step>
</Steps>
