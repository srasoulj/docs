Rumizi has a built-in chat assistant that reads the same menu you see. Use
it when there's too much to scroll through, when you have dietary
questions, or when you just can't decide.

## Where to find it

On a Rumizi menu, tap the **chat icon** in the header. A chat panel
slides in over the menu. You can close it at any time and your cart
stays where it was.

## What you can ask

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="What's vegetarian?" icon="leaf">
    *"Which of the starters are vegetarian?"* The assistant only
    suggests items from the actual menu.
  </Card>
  <Card title="What's spicy?" icon="pepper-hot">
    *"Something spicy but not too heavy?"* It reads item descriptions
    and tags.
  </Card>
  <Card title="What goes well with the pizza?" icon="wine-glass">
    *"What drinks pair with the Margherita?"* It knows the venue's own
    cross-sell suggestions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="I have an allergy" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    *"I'm allergic to nuts. What's safe?"* The assistant reads allergen
    info but always tells you to double-check with your server for
    anything critical.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Building a cart by chat

You can also ask the assistant to add things to your cart:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe what you want">
    *"Add one Margherita, one Caesar salad, and two sparkling waters."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the proposed change">
    The assistant shows you exactly what it's about to add — names,
    quantities, and any modifiers. Nothing touches your cart until you
    confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Apply">
    On confirmation, the items land in your cart. You can keep editing
    from chat or switch back to the normal menu view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The assistant never adds, removes, or changes anything in your cart
without an explicit **Apply** tap from you.
</Note>

## Clearing and starting over

Tap **Clear chat** to wipe the conversation history on your phone. It
doesn't clear your cart — only the chat messages.

## What the assistant can't do

- **Order food for you.** It builds the cart, but the final
  hand-off to the kitchen is up to you and your server.
- **Charge anything.** Rumizi's diner flow doesn't take payments.
- **See other tables.** Each assistant session is scoped to the venue
  you're at and the cart on your phone.

## A word on accuracy

The assistant reads the menu as the venue entered it. It's usually
right, but it's still AI, so for **allergen or dietary questions that
matter to your health, always confirm with your server**. I'd rather you
ask twice than end up with something you can't eat.
