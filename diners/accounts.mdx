You **never** need an account to use a Rumizi menu. Scanning, browsing,
adding to cart, and ordering together all work anonymously.

Some venues offer an optional sign-in for their community features —
posting reviews, saving favorites, seeing past orders. This page explains
how that works.

## One account per venue

Rumizi diner accounts are **per venue**. If you sign up at one restaurant,
that account only works at that restaurant. You're not making a
platform-wide account — you're making a relationship with that one venue.

## Sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the venue's register page">
    While on a venue's menu, tap **Sign in** in the header and pick
    **Create account**. Direct URL:
    `rumizi.com/venue-name/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a password">
    Choose an email and a password just for this venue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    You'll be signed in automatically and can use the venue's community
    features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign in

Back at the same venue? Tap **Sign in** in the header and use the email
and password from when you registered. URL:
`rumizi.com/venue-name/login`.

## What accounts unlock today

- **Community page** — a public feed where the venue and other signed-in
  diners can post. Viewing is open to everyone; **posting** needs a
  signed-in account for that venue.
- **Photo and short text posts** — share what you ordered, tag dishes.

More diner features are on the roadmap (saved favorites, order history,
notifications) but they're not all live yet.

## You can still order anonymously

Signing in is **not** required to order. Cart, order-together, the menu
assistant, translations, and everything else in the menu works
identically whether you're signed in or not. Accounts are purely for the
optional community features.

## Privacy

- Your diner account at one venue is not visible to other venues.
- You can delete your account by asking the venue or emailing
  [hi@rumizi.com](mailto:hi@rumizi.com).
