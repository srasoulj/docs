If your menu isn't loading or your workspace is behaving oddly, here's
how to check and how to get help fast.

## Check your venue first

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your public URL on a fresh browser tab">
    Go to `rumizi.com/your-venue` with no filters or query parameters.
    If it loads normally, the issue is probably local to your device or
    cache, not Rumizi-wide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different device">
    If your phone and laptop both fail, the issue is upstream.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check rumizi.com">
    If `rumizi.com` itself doesn't load either, it's a platform-level
    issue and I'm almost certainly already on it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When you email me, it helps if you include

- The **venue URL** (for example `rumizi.com/bella-napoli`).
- What you were trying to do.
- What happened instead.
- A screenshot or short screen recording if you can.
- What browser and device you're on (iPhone Safari, Chrome on Windows,
  etc.).

The more of those I have, the faster I can fix it.

## Known gaps

A few things are on the roadmap but not in the product yet:

- Auto top-up and in-app card management for AI credit.
- Custom domains (`menu.yourvenue.com`).
- Multi-currency billing.
- A dedicated diner app — for now, the browser experience is the whole
  product for diners.

If one of these is a blocker for you, tell me — I prioritise based on
what real venues ask for.
